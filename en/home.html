Welcome to SRBWiki!

Welcome to SRBWiki! This is a project dedicated to documenting, archiving, and spreading the word about scandals, incidents, and other unfortunate events directly caused by the incompetence and corruption of the Serbian government.

This site is not limited to just the SNS; on the contrary, the problems in Serbia go beyond the SNS name and need to be recorded so they don't repeat. These people are not dependent on a party or ideology, but only look out for their own benefit. If you are interested in contributing to the site, you can edit and create new articles.
